About The School


From class I to X, Aksharasri Kindles the children's Innate Enthusiasm for learning and broadens their pool of knowledge through Audio Visual smart class rooms, educational tours, field trips, regular motivational classes by experts in respective fields and a plethora of curricular and co-curricular activities. Our Virtuoso faculty take pride in shaping the furture citizens by making learning both intellectually stimulating and thoroughly enjoyable. Regular assignments and tests are set diligently to evaluate the performance of each student. As a result, AKSHARASRI has consistently ranked among the best schools in the state, not only in terms of SSC results, but also in terms of state first grades, i.e. 10/10, over the past three years. Even before the grade system our scores 573/600, 568/600 speak of our potential in achieving competitive rank in the district. It is designed to suit the academic needs of the pupils.

Founder & Director

Smt. Nagaratnam

She is a vibrant administrator, an excellent teacher, a stern disciplinarian, the personification of dedication, and the embodiment of motherly love not just for her own children, but for all children.

Above all an impeccable educationist who has been in the field of education for over 30yrs in various capacities. She has been one of the most influential personalities in the city of destiny Rajahmundry.

Smt. Nagaratnam garu stared her saga of successful journey with humble steps as a social teacher in 1981 and since 1986 had been Head Mistress in AMG India International Institutes in Rajahmundry. She founded the prestigious Aksharasri Vidyniketan Now, Aksharasri Smart School in the year 2002 which has ever since been pivotal in shaping the destiny of many a young life to the best of their potential.

Aksharasri has risen to the summit of success in a short period of time, and today it stands as a shining example of - discipline, dedication, and perfection true to its founding ideals and slogan.

Many honours and recognitions were bestowed on her in her honour. She has received numerous honours and medals. Dr. Krishnakanth, the then Governor of Andhra Pradesh, gave her the best teacher award, and Hon'ble Ex. Chief Minister Mr. N. Chandra Babu Naidu gave her the state best teacher award. Sri D. Pallam Raju, Hon'ble Minister of State for Defence, also presented her with the best school award. She has received numerous awards from non-governmental organisations and other organisations, like the Rotary Club and the Lions Club, to name a few. When she met Mother Teresa in person, she constantly claims that she was greatly inspired by her.
